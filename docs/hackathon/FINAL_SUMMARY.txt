                            AGENTS INTEGRATED (5)

POST /reports orchestrates 5 agents in sequence with graceful fallbacks:

  1. ✅ classify()        - Analyzes photos (Gemini Vision)
  2. ✅ getLandmark()     - Location lookup (Google Places)
  3. ✅ routeDepartment() - Maps to department (Local)
  4. ✅ clusterOrCreate() - Groups incidents (Database)
  5. ✅ draftEmail()      - Generates email (Gemini Text)

All agents fail gracefully - reports NEVER block on failures!
